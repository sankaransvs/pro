About Helping Hands

Empowering communities through compassion, education, and sustainable growth since 2010.

Who We Are

The Helping Hands Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities across India. We focus on providing access to education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihood opportunities for those in need.

Our Journey

Founded in 2010 by passionate social workers, Helping Hands began with small community projects in local villages. Today, our work spans multiple states, reaching thousands of families and children with hope and opportunity.

From a handful of volunteers to over 5,000+ active members, our journey continues to inspire change and build resilience in communities nationwide.

Our Vision & Values

We aim to build a world where everyone has equal access to education, healthcare, and the chance to lead a dignified life.

  • Compassion: We care deeply for every individual we serve.
  • Integrity: Transparency and honesty are at the heart of our mission.
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and empower all voices.
  • Sustainability: Long-term impact through responsible action.
